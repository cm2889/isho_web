We are ISHO.

Our mission is to reinvent the use of spaces and create unforgettable experiences for people in their daily lives.

Belief

We believe in being disruptive by design. From conceptualizing and deciding on what new designs to introduce to how we work behind the scenes; all of us at ISHO are truly passionate about bringing a new found appreciation of design to people’s lives with our furniture.

All our collections are inspired by places and our core philosophy is based on creating designs that intrinsically embrace everyday routines and cultures. At ISHO, we make a deliberate effort to study how people use their spaces and what they do in them.

Commitment

From pushing boundaries to create sustainable designs, to our concept studio, and innovation lab, we love nothing more than unlocking the potential of space. Our commitment is steadfast in ensuring our designs are functional and efficient whilst never undermining aesthetics.

Our goal is a simple one and that is to bridge the gap between what is desired in modern living and what is available in the market.
