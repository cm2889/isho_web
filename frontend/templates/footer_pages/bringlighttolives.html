Bring Light To Lives

ISHO's commitment to sustainability goes beyond product development. Recognizing the lack of access to electricity in some rural areas of Bangladesh, ISHO has already donated over 100 solar lamps in Kapashia, Gazipur; Kaligonj, Gazipur; Kishorgonj; and Sherpur, Mymensingh. These lamps have benefitted students, fishermen, small shopkeepers, and housewives, providing a reliable light source for their daily activities. Our lamps symbolize hope, progress, and a commitment to our planet. Together, let's build a greener, more sustainable world.

Join us in our journey towards a greener planet. Donate a lamp today.
