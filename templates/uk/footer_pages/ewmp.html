Empowering Woman Mentorship Programme

Taking a step towards empowering young women, ISHO pledges to strive towards 50/50 gender representation through our progressive Empowering Woman Mentorship Programme, also known as EWMP. The year-long Programme gives the winning candidates the opportunity to work across all areas of the business, to learn from department leaders and contribute to the growth of ISHO in Bangladesh and around the world.

How does it work?

Our Programme allows young Bangladeshi women from a diverse range of backgrounds an opportunity to discover their strengths and passions, at the end of which they’re offered a position in the company with regular mentorship from our Founder and MD, Rayana Hossain. One candidate is recruited each year to shadow the MD in her daily professional activities, to gain relevant exposure, build confidence and work across various departments and projects. After completion of the Programme, the individual is given a choice on which department she would like to join based on her experience and preference.
